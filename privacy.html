Purpose and legal basis

We process B2B enquiry data to answer business enquiries, prepare quotations, confirm product availability and provide requested SDS/TDS/CoA or certification-related documentation. The legal basis is taking steps at the request of the business contact before entering into a contract, legitimate interest in handling B2B correspondence, and—where the form checkbox is used—consent to submit the enquiry.

Data collected

Name, company name, country, email address, product interest, quantity, requested documentation, message content, consent confirmations and basic technical delivery metadata generated by the form delivery process.

Form delivery service

The static website is hosted on GitHub Pages and cannot send server-side email on its own. The contact form is configured to deliver submissions to office@rawmaterialstrade.com through FormSubmit, a static-form delivery service. This means the submitted message is technically processed by the delivery provider before it reaches the Google Workspace mailbox. If the form delivery service is not contractually approved for production use, the site B2B contact should replace it with an approved Google Workspace / Google Apps Script / CRM form endpoint before launch.

Cookies, localStorage and tracking

This website does not load Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Google Fonts, Adobe Fonts or advertising trackers by default. A single localStorage preference may be used only to remember that the essential privacy/cookie notice has been acknowledged. No non-essential tracking cookies should be added without prior consent.

Hosting and logs

The hosting and DNS providers may process standard technical logs such as IP address, requested URL, user agent and timestamp for security, fraud prevention and operation of the website.

Retention

B2B enquiry emails are retained only as long as necessary to handle the enquiry, maintain business correspondence and comply with applicable commercial, accounting and tax obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, data portability and withdrawal of consent by contacting office@rawmaterialstrade.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Hungarian National Authority for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (NAIH): www.naih.hu.

Product information on this website is for initial B2B orientation only. Final suitability, classification, storage, transport, handling and regulatory compliance must be confirmed through official SDS/TDS/CoA and supplier documentation before purchase or use.
